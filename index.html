Quick Start

  1. Grant Camera, Microphone, and Photos access on first launch.
  2. The live viewfinder opens immediately. Bottom toolbar shows:
    • Smart (sparkles): AI describe.
    • Photo (camera): Take a photo.
    • Video (video/stop): Start/stop video.
    • Audio (waveform/mic): Record audio-only.
  3. Swipe up the bottom sheet to open the Gallery.
Haptics confirm starts/stops. If you feel a tap, the action took.

Take Photos

Method 1 — Tap Viewfinder

  1. Tap once on the live viewfinder.
  2. The photo is captured with auto flash when available.
  3. It’s saved to your Photos library. A mini gallery can appear for quick review.

Method 2 — Toolbar Button

  1. Tap the Camera button in the bottom toolbar.
  2. Haptic feedback confirms the shot.
  3. Open the gallery to view, favorite, or share.

Notes: Photos use your current lens and exposure. Flash is set to Auto when supported.

Record Video

Method 1 — Press & Hold

  1. Press and hold on the viewfinder to start recording after a short start cue.
  2. Release to stop.
  3. Video is saved to your Photos library automatically.

Method 2 — Toggle Button

  1. Tap the Video button (shows “video” when idle, “stop” while recording).
  2. Tap again to stop.
  3. A compact gallery preview may pop up with the newest video.

Tip: Bluetooth mics are supported. See Mic selection.

Record Audio-only

  1. Tap the Audio button to start an audio note after the start cue.
  2. Tap again to stop. The app saves an .m4a file.
  3. A system share sheet appears so you can save to Files or share.

Audio uses your chosen input (built-in or Bluetooth). Stereo AAC, 44.1 kHz.

Use AI Vision Smart mode

AI Vision captures a quick photo and streams a description/caption of what it sees.

Describe the Scene

  1. Tap the Sparkles button.
  2. The app snaps a frame and sends it to AI.
  3. Text streams in as it’s generated. Copy or dismiss when done.

Configure AI

  1. Open AI Settings in the app.
  2. Set:
    • API URL: your AI service endpoint
    • API Key: your secret token
    • Model: e.g., a vision-capable model
    • System Prompt: style or constraints you prefer
  3. Return and tap Sparkles again to test.

If streaming stalls, check network and API credentials.

Gallery & Sharing

  1. Swipe up the bottom sheet or tap the gallery prompt after a save.
  2. Browse recent Photos and Videos.
  3. Tap an item to view. Use the share button to export or send.

Videos and photos save to the system Photos library. Audio notes offer a share/export sheet on save.

Controls & Pro Tips

Zoom

  • Pinch on the viewfinder to zoom. A subtle HUD shows the current level.
  • Pause pinching to hide the HUD automatically.

Focus & Exposure

  • Tap to meter and focus on a subject.
  • Move the camera slightly to re-meter if needed.

Mic Selection

  • Open the Mic menu (when visible) to select:
    • System Default (automatic)
    • Available Bluetooth or external mics
  • The app remembers your choice and reapplies it after session changes.

Haptics & Sounds

  • Start and stop actions provide a gentle haptic and short cue.
  • Video and audio start after the cue to avoid capturing the sound.

Settings

  • AI Settings: API URL, Key, Model, System Prompt.
  • Camera: Resolution and stabilization follow device capabilities.
  • Permissions: Ensure Camera, Microphone, and Photos are granted in iOS Settings.

Troubleshooting

  • No camera preview: Check camera permission in iOS Settings.
  • No audio in video: Verify microphone permission and selected input.
  • AI not responding: Confirm network, API URL/Key, and model; try again.
  • Bluetooth mic missing: Toggle Bluetooth, reconnect the device, then reopen mic menu.
  • Photos not saving: Allow Photos access and ensure free storage space.

Privacy

  • On-device capture: Photos and videos are stored locally in your Photos library.
  • AI requests: When using Smart mode, a captured frame is sent to your configured AI endpoint solely for generating a description.
  • Audio notes: Saved locally; you choose where to share/export.
